Hero Classes
------------

- Berserker
  - Strong and crazy

  - Ave HP
  - Ave SP
  - High Att
  - Low Def
  - Low Eva

    > Attack (with axe)
    > Get Mad (ups att/lowers def)
    > Charge
    >

- Fighter
  - Smart and versatile

  - Ave HP
  - Ave SP
  - Ave Att
  - Ave Def
  - Low Eva

    > Attack (with sword)
    > Well-Placed Blow
    > Block (temp eva boost)
    >

- Knight
  - Tough and experienced

  - High HP
  - Low SP
  - Low Att
  - High Def
  - Low Eva

    > Attack (with sword)
    > Dig In (up def)
    >
    >

- Rogue
  - Frail but sly

  - Low HP
  - Ave SP
  - Ave Att
  - Low Def
  - High Eva

    > Attack (with dagger)
    > Sidestep (evade)
    > Debilitate (lower enemy def)
    >

    - Free attack on evade

- Wizard
  - MAGIC

  - Low HP
  - High SP
  - High Att
  - Low Def
  - Low Eva

    > Attack (with staff)
    > Fireball
    > Freeze (Chance to skip enemy's next turn)
    > Daze (lower enemy's att)
